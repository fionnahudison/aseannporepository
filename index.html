ABOUT THE DATABASE

A study originating from the National University of Singapore's Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy (LYKSPP) aims to learn more about the non-profit, non-governmental organisational climate action within the Southeast Asian region. Despite the great climate potential of NPOs in the fight against climate change, there is significantly less information and data pertaining to this sector. This is especially so in the SEA region, thus creating research and knowledge gaps which this study by the Institute of Environment and Sustainability (IES) aims to fill. This repository was created in order to systematically document the existing NPOs in Southeast Asia, with a focus on the ASEAN member countries -- Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, Cambodia, Laos, Brunei, and Myanmar. It aims to determine and consolidate the various information regarding each NPO in these countries, thereby determining their level of climate action.

If you have any comments on the information presented in this repository, do reach out to us via any method of communication on the "Contact Us" section.
